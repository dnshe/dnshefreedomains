## 📖 Introduction
**DNSHE** ([www.dnshe.com](https://www.dnshe.com)) is a non-profit, high-performance domain distribution platform driven by the DNSHE Team. Our mission is to lower the barrier for developers, students, and open-source enthusiasts to build their own digital presence. 

By leveraging enterprise-grade Anycast technology, we provide stable and fast resolution for your projects—completely free of charge.

---

## ✨ Why Choose DNSHE?

### 📅 Industry-Leading Renewal Policy (180 Days)
Unlike other providers that only allow renewal short days before expiry, DNSHE opens a **180-day (6-month) renewal window**. This allows you plenty of time to maintain your domain manually or via automated scripts, ensuring your services never go offline.

### 💎 Enterprise-Grade Anycast Architecture
Our DNS infrastructure is built on a distributed global network:
- **Low Latency**: Millisecond-level resolution speed globally.
- **High Availability**: Redundant nodes ensure 99.9% uptime for your DNS resolution.
- **Full Protocol Support**: Standard support for A, AAAA, CNAME, MX, TXT, NS, SRV, and CAA records.

### 🤖 API-First Management
DNSHE is designed for automation. You can programmatically manage your domains, making it a perfect match for automated testing environments, SaaS platforms, and automated certificate renewals (SSL).

## 🚀 Quick Start

1.  **Register**: Sign up at [dnshe.com](https://www.dnshe.com). No credit card required.
2.  **Claim Domain**: Search for your prefix and link it to your preferred suffix.
3.  **Manage Records**: Add your server IP. Changes propagate globally in seconds.
4.  **Stay Renewed**: Renew your domain within the 180-day window via the dashboard or API.
